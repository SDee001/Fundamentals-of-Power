Why this project exists

The goal is simple: make power systems and scientific computing feel approachable for Electrical Engineering students.

Grounded in real infrastructure

The examples and data are based on an actual institute power system, so equations connect directly to what you see on campus.

From paper to simulation

The SCI Lab tutorials reuse the same ideas from the theory book, but expressed as code, plots and small experiments.

Open, remixable, sharable

Everything is free to download and share. Teachers can adapt it for class; students can extend it for projects and labs.

Use it like a toolkit

  • Skim chapters before a lecture to anchor the concepts.
  • Use SCI Lab examples as templates for your own assignments.
  • Plug in your own measurements from lab or field visits.
  • Adapt figures and scripts for reports or presentations.
